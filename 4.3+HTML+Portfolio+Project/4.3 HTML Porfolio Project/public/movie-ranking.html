My top 3 movies of all time

Oppenheimer

Oppenheimer is a biographical thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, focusing on J. Robert Oppenheimer and the development of the atomic bomb during World War II. The film explores themes of morality, science, and the consequences of technological advancements.

Prestige

The Prestige is a psychological thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, centered around two rival magicians in the late 19th century. The film delves into themes of obsession, sacrifice, and the lengths one will go to achieve greatness.

Aashiqui 2

Aashiqui 2 is a romantic musical drama that tells the story of a fading singer who falls in love with a vibrant young woman, rekindling his passion for music and life. The film explores themes of love, loss, and redemption through its soulful soundtrack and poignant storytelling.
